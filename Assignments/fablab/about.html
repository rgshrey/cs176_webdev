The Watertown High School Fab Lab is a makerspace. It is a place for Watertown students to build, design, code, and create. We have incredible resources that include 3D printers, a laser cutter, vinyl cutters, sewing machines, electronic components, hand tools, and staff who are happy to help you with your next creation. Check our calendar for scheduled activities!
